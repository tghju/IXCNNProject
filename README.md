My CNN tries to differentiate between broccoli and cauliflower. It likely used the differences in shading and the texture of the crown to separate the two otherwise similar vegetables. I later removed the grayscale so that color would also be taken into account. Other than color and minor texture differences, broccoli and cauliflower look almost identical. The images were taken from an already made dataset which provided lots of training data. I have yet to reach 100% accuracy, but usually get 97-99% which is probably good enough. I experimented with various settings, changing epochs, training rate, and batch size. I have not found a setting that makes a huge (positive) difference yet.
